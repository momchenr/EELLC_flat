Product Information

The Ecoremedy® quiescent bed ensures consistent syngas production and low fly ash carryover, assuring continuous and efficient energy production for a wide variety of biomass materials. Patented, precision air control technology combined with infinitely variable feed rates allow Ecoremedy® to customize the amount of carbon in the recovered nutrient to maximize its commercial value based upon real time market conditions for use as a carbon-free fertilizer or carbon-rich designer biochar.  Its modular design offers a wide range of applications to meet your facility's energy requirements.
