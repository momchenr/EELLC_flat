Who We Are

Enginuity Energy LLC is the merger between rem Engineering and Enginuity Energy LLC.  Our foundation is built on 45+ years of power plant design with over 120MWe and 350,000kWt installed.  Our projects include designing, engineering, and implementing energy production solutions to meet the varied energy needs of clients ranging from small agricultural feed mills,  poultry integrators,  industrial manufacturers, food processing plants, institutional campuses, and leading U.S. utility companies.
What We Do

We provide small to medium scale biomass energy and nutrient recovery solutions which convert agricultural and organic-based industrial wastes into usable energy for industrial process steam or power generation.  (24 kWe - 30 MWe)
